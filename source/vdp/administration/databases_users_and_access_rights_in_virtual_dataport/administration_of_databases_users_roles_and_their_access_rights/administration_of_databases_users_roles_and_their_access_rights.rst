=================================================================
Administration of Databases, Users, Roles and Their Access Rights
=================================================================

To manage the databases, users, roles and their privileges of a Virtual
DataPort server, you have to log in as an administrator user. When
accessing the server for the first time, use the default administrator
credentials: login = “admin” and password “admin”.

The following sections describe how to create, modify and delete
databases, users and roles:
